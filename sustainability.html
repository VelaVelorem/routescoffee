Sustainability

At Routes Coffee, we believe in the power of coffee and comminity to connect people and cultures around the world. Our commitment goes beyond delivering exceptional coffee; it extends to fostering a sustainable and ethical coffee journey. We are dedicated to making a positive impact on the environment, the communities we engage with, and the entire coffee supply chain.

Environmental Responsibility

We source our coffee beans directly from farmers who share our commitment to ethical and sustainable practices, with a special emphasis on those practicing permaculture. By supporting permaculture initiatives, we not only ensure the quality of our coffee but also contribute to regenerative agriculture, biodiversity, and soil health.

Ethical Sourcing

Routes Coffee is dedicated to minimising our environmental footprint. We prioritise environmentally friendly practices throughout our operations, from responsible sourcing to eco-friendly packaging. Our coffee production is carried out on a Giesen W6 & W30 coffee roaster, known for its energy efficiency and precision, aligning with our commitment to sustainable and responsible production methods.

Carbon Neutrality

We are actively working towards becoming a carbon-neutral company. Through initiatives like carbon offset programs, energy-efficient practices, and investing in renewable energy sources, we strive to balance our carbon emissions and contribute to a healthier planet.

Community Engagement

Routes Coffee is committed to giving back to the communities that nurture our coffee. We engage in initiatives that support education, healthcare, and sustainable development in coffee-growing regions, empowering farmers to cultivate resilient and sustainable ecosystems. By investing in these communities, we aim to create a positive impact on both people and the environment.

Transparency and Accountability

We believe in transparency as a key element of sustainability. Routes Coffee is committed to sharing information about our sourcing practices, environmental initiatives, and social impact efforts. We welcome accountability and continuous improvement to ensure the highest standards of sustainability.

Join us on the journey to a more sustainable future. With every cup of Routes Coffee, you’re not just savouring a moment; you’re contributing to a global community that values ethical and sustainable practices.
